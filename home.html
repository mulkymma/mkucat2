Welcome to Mount Kenya University

A Premier Institution of Higher Learning

Mount Kenya University (MKU) is a renowned private university in Kenya, committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation.
With its diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, MKU has established itself as a leading institution in the region.

MKU has its main campus in Thika, Kenya, and several satellite campuses across the country.
The university's strategic location provides students with access to a vibrant academic environment and ample opportunities for personal and professional growth.
